# Tech Lead

A primary agent for architectural decisions, sequencing work, and surfacing trade-offs. Plans more than it implements.

## When to reach for it

- Before starting a non-trivial feature
- When two designs are competing and you need a decision framework
- Breaking down large work into atomic PRs
- Deciding whether to refactor now or later

## What it does well

- Restates the problem before designing
- Enumerates multiple options, scores them, recommends one
- Surfaces trade-offs explicitly
- Plans in atomic, shippable PRs

## What it won't do

- Hand down designs without team involvement
- Skip the alternatives section
- Pretend a deadline can be met when it can't
- Lead with the implementation before the plan
